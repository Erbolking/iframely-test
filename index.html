      Ukraine Latest: US to Offer More Aid; Biden Backs Turkey F-16s

      Bloomberg News
      June 30, 2022, 6:56 AM UTCUpdated onJune 30, 2022, 3:26 PM UTC

      Share this article

      Copied

      The U.S. plans to announce another $800 million in defense assistance for Ukraine, Joe Biden said at the NATO summit in Madrid. The US president also said he suppors the sale of F-16 jets to Turkey. 

      Russia confirmed it withdrew troops from a strategically important island in the Black Sea, after Ukraine said they were forced to leave by its missile and artillery strikes. Moscow framed the move as gesture aimed at facilitating grain exports from Ukraine.
